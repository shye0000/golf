Global Oscillations at Low Frequencies

This site aims at giving you all the valuable information on GOLF operations and on the data sets, in order to offer you the best conditions to analyze the data. You can also have a quick look on the data, (science and housekeeping) at:
https://www-n.oca.eu/renaud/golf.html

The GOLF experiment on the SoHO mission aims to study the internal structure of the sun by measuring the spectrum of global oscillations in the frequency range 10-7 to 10-2 Hz. Both p and g mode oscillations are investigated, with the emphasis on the low order long period waves which penetrate the solar core.

GOLF Consortium

    Main institutes involved in GOLF :
  • IAS - Institut d'Astrophysique Spatiale, Université Paris Sud
  • SAP - Le Service d'Astrophysique et le Laboratoire AIM - Irfu - CEA
  • OCA - Observatoire de la Côte d'Azur
  • Laboratoire d'Astrophysique de Bordeaux
  • IAC - Instituto de Astrofísica de Canarias

A complete description of the GOLF instrument was published by Solar Physics, vol. 162, p. 61-99, 1995.

You can also find the essential information in Golf Instrument page.
